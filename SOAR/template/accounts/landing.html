About SOAR

SOAR (Student Organization Activity & Resources) helps students discover, join, and participate in campus organizations. We provide a single place to track events, announcements, and membership across your organizations.

Our mission is to make student engagement simple, inclusive, and discoverable.

Empowering student organizations to connect, collaborate, and grow together.
